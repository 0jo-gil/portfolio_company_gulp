Falling and rising all at the same time

In this project, i wanted to reflect the fact that it is not just making trends, It's a city's lifestyle, emotions, designs. It's worth it to enjoy, live and feel this moments.

Making Someone Unhappy explores an individual’s journey through melancholic depression. The key art for the album depicts a person falling into clouds, alluding to the conceptual direction of rising and falling. The concept is further enforced through imagery of clouds and ocean water across the different touchpoints of the album experience.
